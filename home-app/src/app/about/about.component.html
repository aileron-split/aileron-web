The Company

Aileron d.o.o. (LLC) is a small software engineering company based in Split, Croatia.

Founded in 2016 by, at the time, a senior software engineer and Computer Science graduate Davor Bokun. The company started by offering software development services to clients all around the globe mostly in the domain of web based business applications and advanced product customization interfaces.

Aileron also actively supports and participates in science and technical education events on national level, showing off experimental software products and equipment developed in house, whilst sharing knowledge and experience as well.

We enjoy in breaking new grounds in software applications connecting various technology fields in new ways. Our work is based on great experience in simulation software, games development, open technologies, 3d software, virtual reality and AI, with hands-on experience in robotics, CNC, motion capture, and a lot more...
